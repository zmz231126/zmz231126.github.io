Time work

the overtime premium is the extra rate per hour which is paid and not the whole of the payment for the overtime hours.

if basic rate is $4 per hour and overtime is paid at time and a quarter, eight hours of overtime for the week would be paid the following amount.

Basic pay (8x$4)
overtime premium is 8 x $1
total overtime payment (8 x 5)

labour activity

standard labour hours a standard hour is a “quantity of work”, not a period of time

standard labour hours = actual units of output x standard time per unit.

Efficiency ratio

$$\text{efficiency ratio}=\dfrac{\text{expected hours to make output}}{\text{actual hours taken}}$$

Capacity ratio
$$=\dfrac{\text{actual hours worked}}{\text{budgeted hours}}$$

production volume ratio
$$\text{production volume ratio}=\dfrac{\text{expected hours to make output}}{\text{budgeted hours}}$$

production volume ratio = efficiency ratio x capacity ratio
